Plot Overview

A rare opportunity to acquire a fully documented, premium residential plot in the heart of GRA Phase 3 — one of Port Harcourt's most prestigious and sought-after addresses. This 900 m² parcel sits on a wide, tarred, well-lit street flanked by high-quality residential development, making it an exceptional canvas for your dream home or investment property.

The land is perfectly level, cleared, and ready for immediate development. Underground survey beacons are fully intact. Access to the plot is dual-gated via a secured estate road with 24-hour security presence in the surrounding streets.

The Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) is registered in the vendor's name and available for immediate inspection and transfer. This is a clean, dispute-free title — confirmed through Rivers State Ministry of Lands. Development approval for a detached duplex or multi-unit dwelling is obtainable from the relevant planning authority with this title.
